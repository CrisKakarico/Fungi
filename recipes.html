Recipes

Chanterelle pasta

Cantharellus cibarius pastosus

Chanterelle Mushroom Pasta is a simple Italian dish made with sautéed chanterelle mushrooms, pasta, garlic, olive oil, white wine, and herbs. The pasta is cooked and combined with the sautéed mushrooms, creating a delicious and flavorful meal that showcases the nutty taste of the mushrooms.

Porcini risotto

Boletus edulis risottus

Porcini mushroom risotto is an Italian dish made with sautéed onions, garlic, Arborio rice, white wine, chicken or vegetable broth, and chopped porcini mushrooms. Once cooked, it's finished with Parmesan cheese and butter, creating a rich, comforting, and tasteful dish, perfect for any occasion.

Shiitake stir fry

Lentinula edodes stirfrius

A quick and easy Asian-inspired dish that highlights the umami flavor of shiitake mushrooms. The recipe involves stir-frying sliced shiitake mushrooms with garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and other vegetables of your choice. It can be served over rice or noodles for a satisfying and healthy meal.

Creamy button mushroom soup

Agaricus bisporus cremosus

This recipe is a cozy soup made with fresh button mushrooms. Sauté onions and garlic in butter, add sliced mushrooms, and cook until tender. Add broth, thyme, and cream, and simmer until flavors meld. Puree until smooth, and garnish with additional mushrooms, herbs, or croutons. Perfect for a chilly day.

Portobello burger

Agaricus bisporus burguerosus

A vegetarian burger made with grilled Portobello mushroom caps marinated in balsamic vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic. The mushroom is then topped with melted cheese and served on a toasted bun with lettuce, tomato, and condiments. A healthy and flavorful alternative to traditional burgers.

Creamy morel sauce

Morchella esculenta sauceosus

It's a delicious sauce made with shallots, garlic, and morel mushrooms, combined with chicken or vegetables, heavy cream, and thyme. The sauce is simmered until it thickens and the flavors have melded, creating a tasty sauce that can be served over pasta, chicken, or steak. It's a perfect way to add elegance to any meal.

Black shimeji salad

Hypsizygus marmoreus saladus

This salad is a simple yet flavorful dish that combines fresh greens with sautéed black shimeji mushrooms, tomatoes, and crumbled feta cheese. The tangy dressing made with lemon juice, olive oil, and mustard adds a zesty flavor to the recipe. It's a perfect light lunch that's easy to make and impresses your guests.
